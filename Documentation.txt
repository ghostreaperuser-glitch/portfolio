# GhostR Software 
**The academic and professional platform built in Uganda, for Uganda — and beyond.**

---

## What Is GhostR Software?

GhostR Software is a web platform that connects students, lecturers, job seekers, and businesses in one place. It started as a portfolio site and is growing into a full ecosystem — academic tools, verified identities, job listings, AI research assistants, and more.

The site works on **every device** — phone, tablet, laptop — and can be **installed as an app** without going to any app store.

---

## Installing the App (PWA)

You don't download it from Google Play or the App Store. You install it straight from your browser in seconds.

| Your Device | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Android** | Open the site in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" |
| **iPhone / iPad** | Open in Safari → tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" |
| **Windows / Mac** | Open in Chrome or Edge → click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar |

Once installed, it opens full-screen like a native app — no browser bar, works partially offline.

## For Lecturers 

- **Upload up to 3 documents per semester** — notes, coursework, reading guides
- **Earn a verified badge** — your identity is confirmed by GhostR, giving your materials more trust and visibility
- **Reach students across departments** — not just your class, anyone on the platform can benefit from your work

---

## The Verified Badge ✅

The blue verified badge (`✓`) next to a name means GhostR has confirmed that person's academic identity. It is not automatic — it is earned.

**Why it matters:**
- Students know the notes they're downloading are from a real, confirmed lecturer
- Lecturers with badges get more visibility and credibility on the platform
- In the future, verified individuals will be prioritized in job matching, partnerships, and platform features
- It protects the community from fake accounts and misinformation

## Coming Soon 

GhostR Software is just getting started. Here's what's being built:

**More Institutions**
The platform will expand to other universities, secondary schools, and vocational/technical institutions across Uganda and the region. Each institution will have its own verified contributor space.

**Verified Businesses**
Local businesses near campuses will be able to create verified profiles — making it easier for students to find internships, part-time work, and partnerships.

**Job Board**
Students who are job-ready will post profiles. Employers and businesses will post opportunities. The two will meet here — no middleman, no fees.

**AI Research Agents**
Built-in AI assistants that students can use to research topics, summarize documents, and get answers — all without leaving GhostR. Think of it as having a study partner available 24/7.

**Software & Resource Downloads**
GhostR will host curated download links for useful software, tools, systems, and learning resources — vetted and organised so you're not hunting across the internet.
